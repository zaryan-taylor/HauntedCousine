# AI-Only-Project



im going to make a cookie clicker inspired game in  which the target you have to click moves every so often, with upgrades the target lifetime, size and point reward can be increased.





you are (insert name), starting a new restaurant in a remote village, upon entering the place you hired, you find out the place is haunted making any food prepared teleport around the room, the villages tell you about a myth oif a ghost haunting the building, with its dying wish to make a proper restaurant. the ghost will challance you until you succeed in what the ghost couldn't do.







you have to click a dish that moves every 5 seconds orso, granting money in return.

upgrades include:

\-tools (spatula, ect) increasing the lifetime of a dish.

\-equipment (better stove) increasing the target size.

\-dishes (better/more luxurious dishes) increasing the reward for clicking the dish.
