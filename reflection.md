# reflection

- what project did i choose and why?
i chose to make a clickergame centered around restaurant dishes. the idea was inspired from a webtoon i was reading at the time in which a renowned chef lost his sense of taste and went to the countryside to take a rest, from there he meets and cooks for new people and leaned to be a better person, i took inspiration from the countryside restaurant idea because i really enjoyed reading it and added a haunted ghost that will try to make you fail in to make it more interesting.


- what did i expect to do?
i expected to have a homescreen in which you can start the game nad leave it, i also thought i could have atleast 5 dishes done and have atleast 3 mins of gameplay without it feeling boring/forced to play, i wanted to have a background that looked like a rundown kitchen to resonate more with the haunted idea.


-how did it actually go?
i failed miserably at the expectations i had (i overscoped), altough i got 3 dishes done and functional i still had alot more i wanted to get done, i mainly got distracted really hard due to my first few llessons of fixing the repository, after that i continiously switched on and off between getting distracted and working well, resulting in not having much done and feeling as if the project was inadequite even when i had moments where i worked really well for a long time.


- what went well?
i got the sprites for 2 dishes done and made the dishes functional.
durign the moments i was able to focus without distractions i did work well and was happy with what i did.


  
- what could i improve on?
  manage my time and not overscope my ideas and what i expect from myself, to re prioritize more important things (functionality over visuals), get a better grip and focus better without distracting myself. i noticed my prompts to chatgpt were really short and didnt have enough details in them causing me to use multiple prompts to fix 1 mistake.

  
- what is my opinion on this project?
  the idea of using only AI did make me feel quite uncomfortable because i do not like to use it at all, from my view using AI too much will result in a person thinking less for  themselves and started to rely on it for simple things. my mother has become a victom of this, making me even more hesistant in using it unless i truely cant figure it out myself. the results from the project also didnt really satisfy me, i wanted to get alot more done but i mismanaged my time and got too distracted too much.
